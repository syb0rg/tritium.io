About Tritium

Tritium is a compact, fast run-time synthesis engine primarily designed for both small embedded systems and large servers.

One use for Tritium is as an important fuel for controlled nuclear fusion. Based off of that idea, Tritium was created to fuel other projects with a simple, yet powerful, speech sythesizer.
